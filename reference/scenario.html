What is Scenario?

Scenario is an AI platform specifically designed for generating high-quality, consistent game assets. It allows users to train their own AI models on their unique art style, ensuring that all generated assets fit seamlessly into their game world.

Common Use Cases

  • Consistent Character Sprites: Generate multiple poses and expressions for a character in the same style.
  • Game Props & Items: Quickly create a wide variety of in-game items like weapons, potions, and furniture.
  • Isometric Assets: Produce assets for isometric or 2.5D games.
  • Marketing & Icons: Create icons and marketing materials that match the game's art style.

Getting Started: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Access the Tool: Sign up or log in on the Scenario website.
  2. Create a Model: Upload your existing art to train a custom AI model on your style.
  3. Generate Images: Once the model is trained, use text prompts to generate new assets in your style.
  4. Refine and Edit: Use the built-in tools to make adjustments to the generated images.
  5. Export Assets: Export the finished assets for use in your game engine.

Tips for Effective Prompts

  • Be Specific About the Asset: Clearly define the asset you need (e.g., "side-scrolling sprite of a goblin warrior, holding a rusty sword").
  • Reference Your Model: Ensure you are using your custom-trained model for generation.
  • Define the View: Specify the perspective (e.g., "top-down view," "isometric").
  • Iterate on Prompts: Start with a simple prompt and add more detail to refine the output.
