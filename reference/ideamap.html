What is Ideamap?

Ideamap is a visual workspace designed for brainstorming where teams can collaborate on ideas and leverage AI to boost their creativity. Unlike traditional mind mapping or note-taking tools, Ideamap combines collaborative brainstorming with intelligent AI assistance to help teams generate, organize, and develop ideas more effectively.

Use Cases

  • Team Brainstorming Sessions: Create a shared visual workspace where team members can contribute ideas in real-time, with AI helping to expand and refine concepts.
  • Product Development: Map out product features, user stories, and roadmaps collaboratively, using AI to identify gaps and suggest improvements.
  • Strategic Planning: Visualize business strategies, market opportunities, and competitive landscapes with your team, letting AI surface insights and connections.
  • Creative Projects: Develop creative concepts for marketing campaigns, content strategies, or design projects with collaborative ideation powered by AI.
  • Problem Solving: Break down complex problems visually, invite team input, and use AI to generate potential solutions and approaches.

Getting Started

  • Create a Workspace: Start by creating a new workspace for your brainstorming session. Give it a clear title that reflects your project or goal.
  • Add Your First Ideas: Begin adding ideas to your visual canvas. You can type them directly, and the tool will organize them spatially for easy viewing and connection-making.
  • Invite Collaborators: Share your workspace with team members so everyone can contribute ideas simultaneously. Real-time collaboration keeps everyone aligned.
  • Engage the AI: Use Ideamap's AI features to expand on your ideas, generate related concepts, or get suggestions for new directions. The AI can help break through creative blocks.
  • Organize Visually: Drag and connect ideas to show relationships, group related concepts together, and create a visual structure that makes sense for your project.
  • Iterate and Refine: As ideas develop, use the collaborative features to get feedback, refine concepts, and build on what's working. The AI can help synthesize multiple ideas into stronger concepts.

Tips

  • Start Broad, Then Focus: Begin with open-ended brainstorming to capture all possibilities, then use AI assistance to help narrow down and refine the most promising ideas.
  • Use Visual Organization: Take advantage of the spatial canvas to create clear groupings and hierarchies. Visual organization helps teams see patterns and relationships more easily.
  • Leverage AI for Diversity: When the team hits a creative block, ask the AI to suggest alternative perspectives or unconventional approaches to stimulate new thinking.
  • Document Decisions: As ideas evolve, keep notes on why certain directions were chosen or rejected. This context is valuable for future reference and onboarding new team members.
  • Regular Check-ins: For ongoing projects, schedule regular sessions to revisit and update your Ideamap. The visual format makes it easy to see progress and identify what needs attention.
