What is Teachable Machine?

Teachable Machine is a web-based tool that makes creating machine learning models fast, easy, and accessible to everyone. You can train a computer to recognize your own images, sounds, and poses.

Common Use Cases

  • Image Classification: Train a model to classify images (e.g., cats vs. dogs).
  • Sound Classification: Train a model to classify sounds (e.g., different bird songs).
  • Pose Detection: Train a model to recognize different body poses.
  • Educational Tool: A great tool for learning the basics of machine learning.

Getting Started: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Open the website: Go to the Teachable Machine website.
  2. Create a new project: Choose between an image, sound, or pose project.
  3. Gather your data: Upload your own images, or record sounds or poses.
  4. Train your model: Click the "Train Model" button to train your model.
  5. Test and export: Test your model and export it for use in your projects.

Tips for Effective Use

  • Use diverse data: The more diverse your training data, the better your model will be.
  • Use a balanced dataset: Try to have a similar number of examples for each class.
  • Experiment with the settings: Try different settings to see how they affect your model.
