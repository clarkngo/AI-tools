What is RunwayML?

RunwayML is a comprehensive online platform for artists and creators that provides a suite of powerful AI "magic tools" focused on video and image manipulation. It started as a tool for artists to use machine learning models and has evolved into a full-featured, web-based content creation suite, most famous for its video generation capabilities.

Common Use Cases

  • Text-to-Video: Generate short video clips from a simple text description (e.g., "a drone shot flying through a futuristic city").
  • Video Editing: Automatically remove backgrounds from videos, paint out unwanted objects, or track the motion of an object in a scene.
  • Image Generation: Create and expand images using text prompts, similar to other image generators.
  • AI Training: For advanced users, Runway allows you to train your own custom AI models on your own data.

Getting Started: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Create an Account: Sign up on the RunwayML website. You will get a certain number of free credits to start experimenting.
  2. Explore the AI Magic Tools: The dashboard presents a wide array of tools. A good one to start with is "Text to Video (Gen-2)."
  3. Generate Your First Video: In the Gen-2 tool, type a prompt describing the video clip you want to create. Be descriptive about the subject, action, and style. Click "Generate."
  4. Edit an Existing Video: Upload one of your own video clips. Try using the "Remove Background" tool to instantly create a green-screen effect, or the "Inpainting" tool to brush over and remove an object from the scene.
  5. Export Your Creation: Once you are happy with the result, you can export the video or image file.

Tips for Effective Use

  • Start with a Clear Goal: Know what you want to achieve. Are you generating a new clip, or editing an existing one? Runway has a specific tool for almost every task.
  • Keep Video Prompts Simple: Text-to-video is still a new technology. Prompts with a clear subject and a simple action (e.g., "a man walking on a beach") work better than complex scenes with multiple actions.
  • Use Image Prompts for Gen-2: You can upload a starting image along with your text prompt to have more control over the composition and style of the generated video.
  • Chain Tools Together: A powerful workflow is to generate a clip with Gen-2, then take that clip into the editor to remove the background, then use the motion tracking tool on the result.
