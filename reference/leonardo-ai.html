What is Leonardo AI?

Leonardo AI is a powerful and versatile AI image generation platform designed for creating high-quality art, illustrations, and assets for creative projects. It offers a suite of tools and fine-tuned models to give users granular control over the final output.

Common Use Cases

  • Game Assets: Create character sprites, environments, and items for video games.
  • Concept Art: Generate visual ideas for characters, creatures, and worlds.
  • Marketing & Advertising: Produce unique and eye-catching visuals for campaigns.
  • Product Prototyping: Visualize product designs and concepts quickly.

Getting Started: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Access the Tool: Go to the Leonardo AI website and sign up or log in.
  2. Explore Models: Browse the platform's fine-tuned models to find a style that fits your vision.
  3. Write a Prompt: In the image generation tool, describe the image you want to create. Be descriptive!
  4. Select a Model & Style: Choose the model and any specific style settings.
  5. Generate & Refine: Generate the image. Use tools like "Image to Image" or "Alchemy" to refine the result.

Tips for Effective Prompts

  • Use Strong Keywords: Include keywords that define the subject, style, and composition (e.g., "photorealistic portrait of a futuristic soldier, cinematic lighting, 8k").
  • Leverage Negative Prompts: Use the negative prompt field to specify what you don't want to see (e.g., "blurry, cartoon, text").
  • Control Composition: Mention camera angles and shot types (e.g., "wide-angle shot," "from below").
  • Specify Artists or Styles: Include artist names or art styles to guide the output (e.g., "in the style of Van Gogh").
