What is Explain Paper?

Explain Paper is an AI-powered tool designed to make academic and scientific papers easier to understand. It allows you to upload a research paper, highlight confusing text, and get a simple explanation, helping to break down the barriers of dense, technical jargon.

Common Use Cases

  • Students: Quickly get up to speed on required reading for a course, especially in technical fields.
  • Researchers Entering a New Field: Understand the foundational papers in a new area of study without getting bogged down by unfamiliar terminology.
  • Journalists and Science Communicators: Accurately report on new scientific findings by ensuring they fully understand the source material.
  • Curious Minds: Explore interesting scientific topics without needing a Ph.D. to understand the papers.

Getting Started: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Upload a Paper: Go to the Explain Paper website. The first step is to upload a PDF of the research paper you want to read.
  2. Highlight Confusing Text: Once the paper is loaded, read through it as you normally would. When you encounter a sentence, paragraph, or even a mathematical equation that you don't understand, simply highlight it with your mouse.
  3. Get an Explanation: After highlighting the text, an AI-powered panel will appear. It will provide a simplified explanation of the selected text, defining jargon and clarifying complex ideas.
  4. Ask Follow-up Questions: The explanation is interactive. If the simplified version is still unclear, you can ask follow-up questions in a chat-like interface to dive deeper into the concept.
  5. Change the Difficulty: You can often specify the level of the explanation, for example, asking for it to be explained to a 5th grader, an undergraduate, or a fellow researcher from a different field.

Tips for Effective Use

  • Don't Highlight Everything: Use the tool strategically. Try to understand the paper on your own first, and use Explain Paper for the specific sections that are blocking your comprehension.
  • Focus on Methodology and Results: These are often the densest parts of a paper. Use the tool to clarify the statistical methods used or the implications of the results.
  • Combine with Other Tools: Use Explain Paper to understand the text, and a tool like Perplexity AI to find related papers or definitions of key concepts.
  • Check the Original Context: The AI's explanation is a great starting point, but always refer back to the original text to ensure the nuance of the author's meaning is not lost.
